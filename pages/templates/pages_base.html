About Us

We are a team of engineers, designers and developers striving to provide solutions that simplify and modernize the construction process. The 'Orit Construction' web application brings all your ongoing construction projects to a central database and provides you with a sleek web interface to easily follow-up, update and receive notification about the status of the projects regardless of your location. All you need is an internet connection and a browser.

Great Features

Orit Construction web app have a wide selection of great features to help you organize and administer your construction projects

Projects

Easily follow-up, update and be notified of your current and past construction projects status.

Insurance

Track the status and deadlines of your project's Advance Bond Guaranttee and Peformance Bond Guaranttee

Payments

Follow-up ongoing project payments and be the first to be notified regarding their change of status.  Coming Soon!

Variations

Track status of project variations and compare & contrast approved variations against submitted variations.

Time Claims

Track status of project time extension claims and compare & contrast approved time extensions against submitted/requested time extensions.

Subcontracts

Track all your sub-contractors' activities, current status, progress and payments.  Coming Soon!
